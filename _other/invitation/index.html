Welcome to a New Year!

Granite Lion Cellars Wine Event and Beach Party

Wine, Song, and Surf.

Granite Lion Cellars has opened its tasting room, completed plans for expansion of the vineyard, and developed an outdoor picnic area complete with a hillside waterfall and canyon stream. More improvements are on the way in 2015! We appreciate the support and interest you have given our endeavors in the last year. Please join us in celebration on January 30th to February 1st. All-American Beach Party Friday January 30th at 4:00 p.m. Granite Lion Cellars welcomes you to an All-American Beach Party at the future site of the Imperial Beach Hotel and Resort. Granite Lion Cellars wine will be poured as the sun sets over the ocean and as specialty selections come off the grill. 1080 Seacoast Drive, Imperial Beach, CA 91932 Breakfast on the Beach Saturday January 31 Start the day with breakfast on the beach. Pancakes, waffles, fresh fruit, pastries and coffee will be served against a landscape of beach sand and white-capped waves. 1080 Seacoast Drive, Imperial Beach, CA 91932 ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ Granite Lion Cellars Wine Tasting Saturday January 31 Wine tasting is open at the winery at 11:00 a.m. on Saturday. As part of the celebration of our first "Pick-Up Party," we invite you to bring your favorite selection of Granite Lion wine out to the Grove to pair with a selection of food as you enjoy hiking, winery tours, and visiting the site for the new tasting room and cave. Club members can come pick up your quarterly shipment of wine. There will be live guitar music and singing in the Grove. 100 yards south of 2824 Jamul Highlands Road, Jamul, CA 91935 Sunday Beach Brunch Sunday February 1st Complete the weekend with breakfast on the beach Sunday morning from 9:00 to 11:00 a.m. If you have time, return to the Grove to enjoy award winning wine in a classic California wine setting. 1080 Seacoast Drive, Imperial Beach, CA 91932 Let us know if you have questions. Thank you for being a part of our passion for good wine and we are looking forward to celebrating with you. See you there!
