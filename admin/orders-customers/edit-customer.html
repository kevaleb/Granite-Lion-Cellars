How to edit customer details

  • Log in to OrderDesk.com
  • Go to the "FC Customers" page
    • (look in the "Custom Tools Section" of the left navigation.
  • From there you'll be able to edit email, password and addresses.
    • Note: You can NOT update a customers payment information here.
    • Note: This will not update the customers information in the email client. You will need to do that separately.
