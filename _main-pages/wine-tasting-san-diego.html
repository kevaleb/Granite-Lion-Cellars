We Are Open

Friday, Saturday, Sunday & Major Holidays

11:00 AM to 5:00 PM
( or by appointment 619-933-3268 )

Tastings are available at the production facility. Come enjoy our award winning red and white wines, and get a taste from the barrel of what is coming.

Tastings are complimentary for wine club members, and we will discount the fee to the purchase of your first bottle for all visitors. We will be open Friday, Saturday, and Sunday from 11 a.m. to dusk.  We invite you to call us for  appointments with larger groups, and we will meet you there.

You can find us at 2801 Jamul Highlands Road.

We look forward to seeing you at the winery, remember to bring a picnic!
