Come Visit Our Vineyard in San Diego Wine Country

Just 14 miles from the waterfront our San Diego Vineyard is an easy drive from the city.

The Granite Lion Vineyard is nestled in the San Diego foothills. Cradled against the sun and fed by the evening sea breezes, the grapes demonstrate the depth of ideal growing conditions.

We are the first of the wineries on the Highway 94 Wine Trail

The Wineries on Highway 94 is a growing affiliation of commercial growers and wineries near san diego who have a shared vision of the San Diego East County as being an ideal location for the establishment of exceptional wine grape vineyards. We are now demonstrating that the high quality fruit produced by these vines can in fact become world class wines. Now this vision is a reality as several of the wines vinted here have recently been not only recognized but have received awards in international competition.
